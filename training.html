Working with Organisations

Training

Workshops for teams who are ready to change how they actually work. Sessions are tailored to your audience and grounded in real experience.

Workshop catalogue

Neurodiversity in the Workplace

What neurodiversity means in practice, how neurodivergent employees experience your organisation, and what needs to change. For whole-team or leadership audiences. Half or full day.

Conscious Inclusion

Moving beyond implicit bias training. A practical session on how inclusion behaviours show up day-to-day and how teams can build habits that stick. For whole teams. Half day.

Conscious Hiring

Redesigning your recruitment process to stop screening out good candidates. For hiring managers, HR and talent teams. Half day, with optional process audit add-on.

Allyship in Action

What meaningful allyship looks like for disabled and neurodivergent colleagues — beyond awareness days. For employee resource groups and general staff. Two hours to half day.

Supporting Disabled Employees

Practical skills for line managers: how to start the conversation, work through adjustments, and create the conditions for people to actually tell you what they need. For people managers. Half day.

Bespoke Sessions

All workshops can be adapted for your context. I also design sessions from scratch for specific challenges — culture change programmes, leadership offsites, ERG events and more. Get in touch to discuss.

How it works

1

Needs assessment

We talk through your audience, your goals and what you've already tried. I ask questions most training providers skip.

2

Tailored design

I adapt the session content, examples and exercises for your specific organisation, sector and audience. Nothing generic.

3

Delivery

In-person or online, half day to full day. I facilitate in a way that's accessible to neurodivergent participants from the start.

4

Follow-up resources

Participants leave with practical takeaways. I can also provide follow-up resources, action planning tools and manager guides.
