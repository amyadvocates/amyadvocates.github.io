Your First Reflection

This reflection is just for you. You don't have to share it anywhere, and there's no right answer. The goal is to start recognising what works for you.

Think about one job, role, project, course, or work situation that looked fine from the outside — but cost you more than it should have.

It might be something you're dealing with right now, or something from years ago. Just one example you can hold in mind.

If this brings up something too big to grapple with emotionally, choose a smaller or more neutral example. You do not have to start with the hardest thing.
